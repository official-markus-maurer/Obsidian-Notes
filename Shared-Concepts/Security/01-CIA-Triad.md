# The CIA Triad

The core principles of information security.

## Confidentiality
-   **Definition**: Only authorized users can access the data.
-   **Techniques**: Encryption, Access Control Lists (ACLs), MFA.
-   **Example**: HTTPS encrypts your credit card number.

## Integrity
-   **Definition**: Data has not been tampered with.
-   **Techniques**: Hashing (SHA-256), Digital Signatures.
-   **Example**: Downloading software and checking the checksum.

## Availability
-   **Definition**: Data is accessible when needed.
-   **Techniques**: Redundancy, Load Balancing, DDoS Protection.
-   **Example**: Google Search being up 99.99% of the time.
